Demigod Forge

A hustler like you with a grandiose vision of becoming a high achieving leader has a ton of potential, you deserve to get the maximum out of it!


As a hustler you have met the real face of human nature. The way your authority and attractiveness is determined by your physical performance. You see how the results of fitpreneurs is skyrocketing. You have tried following thier diets and workout routines to become one of these top-performing gentleman. But the truth is that neither of these are exactly tailored to you. You might even be disappointed and confused by the many sources of fitness routines available. Your business is the pure extension of who you are. Would you hire the extension of a weak, skinny, sick or overweight person?

You know you cannot keep up with the top performers until you become one of the top 1% physically. You will not have their energy, authority, sales, attractiveness and efficiency until you achieve those 2 things which makes them different:

  1. Strength
  2. Mindset

The solution lies in the most basic factor of our existence: Human evolution. The survival of the fittest ensures natural selection based on our physical strength. The same applies for business, relationships and family. The stronger you are, the bigger pack you lead. You become a Mythical figure by unleashing the power your body is capable of. As a strength trainer I am your mentor on your journey to become one of the strongest and most influential people of our species.

Beyond achieving superhuman strength and muscle mass with me you will 100X the power of your Mindset. You will learn the ancient Demigod way of thinking designed to conqouer the space of business, athleticism and influence. Your way of becoming a Divine human starts with training your Mindset.

I’m driven by the passion of helping people with this because I know it’s possible to achieve a jacked, strong and healthy physique naturally. This will make you 3X more efficient in your hustles, personal life and competitive carreer. I have met and coached so many great guys like you who all have the foundation for becoming a Demigod but still struggle to get there. Forget about the struggles! My mission is to help you.

This is what the service includes and what it will do for you;

  • Bridge the gap between you & your dream self
  • Make you more confident than ever - say goodbye to insecurities once & for all
  • Give you complete clarity how you will achieve & keep your goal physique
  • Sculpt a living Demigod out of your frame
  • Teach you how to utilize healthy habits which contribute to your finances, wellbeing, strength and longevity

And this is how we will do it;

  • Using the platform ‘Notion’ for content and communication
  • I will ask you questions about your physique, lifestyle and goals
  • You will receive a workout plan and nutrition guide according to your needs
  • You will record one set of each exercise for me to check your form and performance every week
  • We have weekly calls to discuss the week’s results, specifics that came up over the week and how to refine the process

You will learn how to

  • Gain influence through appearance
  • Establish authority by physical strength
  • Do 2X as much in half the time
  • Program your training prefectly so you won’t NEED me anymore
